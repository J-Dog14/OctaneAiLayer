## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Luke Bryant prioritizes addressing his significant deficits in hand angular velocity, lead leg ground force, and elbow flexion at max external rotation, along with general shotput acceleration power. The P0 and P1 sessions focus on feel, connection, and gradual intent, while the P2 session pushes for maximum intent expression and targeted inefficiency correction. As Luke is in the 'Velocity Phase', higher intent is permissible, but the weekly layout for a starter with a Saturday game follows an in-season cadence, meaning the game itself serves as the high-intent event, with plyos supporting recovery and pre-game priming rather than adding a separate P2 day.
