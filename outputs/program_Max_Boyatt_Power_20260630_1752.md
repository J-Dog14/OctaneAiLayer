## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Max Boyatt exhibits key deficits in elbow flexion at max ER (arm path), pelvis rotation stop time (hip drive/separation), and lead leg impulse (lead leg block). The prescribed three-day plyo cycle directly targets these inefficiencies, leveraging the 'Velocity Phase' to emphasize intent and power. The P0 and P1 sessions prioritize feel, connection, and gentle pattern reinforcement, while the P2 session escalates intent and explosiveness with drills specifically chosen to correct the kinematic sequence and lead leg action. This cycle provides Max with structured progression, balancing recovery with aggressive development, and preparing him for his Saturday game day.
