## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Isaiah Pinero's primary deficits highlight a critical need to improve vertical force generation through the lead leg at front foot contact. This three-day plyo cycle directly targets this inefficiency, alongside overall hip drive and intent, which are secondary needs. Given he is a starter in the Velocity Phase, the program balances high-intent work with necessary recovery, preventing overtraining while aggressively addressing his mechanical gaps. The cycle progresses from tissue restoration and feel (P0) to moderate intent (P1) and culminates in maximal intent expression and specific lead-leg patterning (P2), ensuring comprehensive development.
