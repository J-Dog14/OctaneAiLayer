## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Barron Mcconnell, a High School Starter in the Velocity Phase, presents with primary deficits in pelvis angular velocity at foot contact and elbow flexion at max external rotation, alongside general Proteus power/acceleration deficiencies. This three-day plyo cycle is designed to progressively address these. P0 focuses on recovery and re-establishing foundational movement feel. P1 introduces light intent and begins targeted work on hip drive and arm path. P2 is the primary work day, emphasizing maximal intent to correct kinematic sequence and arm path inefficiencies, leveraging the Velocity Phase's allowance for higher intensity.
