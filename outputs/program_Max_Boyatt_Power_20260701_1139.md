## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Max Boyatt, a college starter in the Velocity Phase, focuses on addressing his dominant deficits in elbow flexion at maximum external rotation, pelvis rotational stop time, and lead leg impulse. The P0 and P1 sessions prioritize feel, arm path, and foundational hip drive/connection, while the P2 session aggressively targets lead leg block and power output deficiencies with max intent drills. Given Max is in the Velocity Phase, all P0, P1, and P2 levels are permissible without downgrade, allowing for progressive intensity throughout the week to maximize both recovery and intent education.
