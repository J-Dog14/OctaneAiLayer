## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Ethan Dumar's cycle primarily targets his significant deficit in lead leg vertical force production, aiming to improve his ability to brace and transfer energy through the lead leg. A secondary focus addresses his horizontal abduction mobility to enhance hip rotation and separation. The Velocity Phase allows for high-intensity plyo work, enabling aggressive drill selection at P2 to drive intent and force development. P0 and P1 days provide crucial recovery and pattern reinforcement, ensuring durability and consistent feel throughout the weekly cycle, especially for a starter's demanding schedule.
