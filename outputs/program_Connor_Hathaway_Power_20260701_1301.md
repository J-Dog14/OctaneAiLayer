## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor's plyo cycle targets his significant Proteus D2 extension deficits, which highlight a need for improved rotational power and acceleration into extension. The sessions are designed to foster a strong 'hip drive' and 'intent/effort' feel, progressing from low-intensity recovery to high-intent expression. As Connor is in the Velocity Phase and a Starter, the in-season cadence is applied, emphasizing recovery post-game and allowing for focused, high-intent work without stacking P2 on game days.
