## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Simon Martone, a college starter in the Velocity Phase, focuses primarily on addressing his top pitching mechanics deficits: lead knee stability at release and pelvis obliquity at foot contact. These indicate a need for improved lead leg block and more efficient hip drive/rotation, which also links to his Proteus power deficits. The 'Velocity Phase' annual phase allows for high-intensity work up to P3, so no downgrades from the suggested cadence are necessary. Each session builds upon the last, progressively increasing intent while targeting these key movement patterns, allowing Simon to cultivate better feel for his lower half and express greater power.
