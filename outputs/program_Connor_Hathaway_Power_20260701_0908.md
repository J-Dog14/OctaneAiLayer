## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor's dominant deficits in Proteus (D2 extension velocity/acceleration) and force plate metrics (jump impulse/velocity) point to a need for improved explosive lower body and trunk extension power. The cycle is designed to progressively address these through focused hip drive and kinematic sequencing, with a feel goal of enhanced hip drive and intent. Given Connor is a starter in the Velocity Phase, the weekly cadence permits up to P2 on game day and P1 mid-week, with P0 for mandatory recovery. No phase-based downgrades were necessary for these levels, allowing for direct application of the P0/P1/P2 framework to build power and transfer it to the mound.
