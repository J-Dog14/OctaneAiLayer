## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Brett Jordan's plyo cycle prioritizes addressing his significant deficits in prone hamstring mobility and CMJ vertical power, which collectively indicate a need for improved hip drive, pelvis control, and lead leg block efficiency. The Velocity Phase allows for higher intensity, so P2 days push intent and aggressive correction, while P0 and P1 days focus on establishing feel and gradually building movement quality. The drill selection across the cycle emphasizes lower-half engagement, rotational power, and dynamic lead leg action to bridge these biomechanical gaps and enhance overall kinematic sequencing and power transfer.
