## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Connor Wong is designed to directly address his primary pitching deficits: poor trunk lateral flexion and pelvis obliquity at release, a lack of explosive shotput acceleration (rotational power), and diminished humerus angular velocity. The Velocity Phase allows for maximal intent, so the P2 sessions are geared for aggressive correction of these rotational and power deficits, focusing on hip drive and overall intent. The P0 and P1 days provide crucial recovery and progressive exposure to movement patterns, ensuring consistent reinforcement of a clean arm path and efficient kinetic sequencing without overtaxing the system. The cycle prioritizes building rotational power and arm speed, which are key to success in the Velocity Phase.
