## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Max Boyatt targets his primary pitching deficits: inefficient elbow flexion at max external rotation, suboptimal pelvis rotation stop time, and weak lead leg vertical impulse. The cycle progressively emphasizes arm path fluidity, hip drive and rotational sequencing, and lead leg bracing across increasing intensities. As Max is in the Velocity Phase, all P0, P1, and P2 levels are permissible, allowing for aggressive correction at higher intensities while maintaining crucial recovery and feel at lower levels. The drill selection prioritizes movement patterns that directly improve his kinematic sequence and power output, aiming to transfer these gains to mound velocity.
