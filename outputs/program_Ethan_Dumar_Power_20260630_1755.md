## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Ethan Dumar's primary deficit is a significant lack of lead leg vertical force production, affecting his ability to effectively block and transfer energy up the chain. His secondary deficit is related to horizontal abduction mobility. This three-day plyo cycle directly addresses these by progressively incorporating drills focused on lead leg engagement, hip drive, and overall intent. As Ethan is in the Velocity Phase, all plyo levels (P0, P1, P2) are permitted, allowing for aggressive intent development. The cycle moves from foundational recovery and arm path work (P0), through dynamic loading and rhythm (P1), to high-intent, lead-leg dominant drills (P2) to close his biomechanical gaps and enhance power.
