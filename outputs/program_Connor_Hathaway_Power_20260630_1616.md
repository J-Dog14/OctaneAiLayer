## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor's dominant deficit is in Proteus D2 extension velocity and acceleration, indicating a critical need to improve hip drive and the speed of force transfer. This three-day plyo cycle directly addresses this by progressively increasing the intent and specificity of drills targeting explosive hip rotation and lower-half power. The P0 and P1 days focus on establishing proper movement patterns and feel, particularly for hip rotation, while the P2 day pushes maximum intent for power development, aligning with his 'Velocity Phase' annual training goal.
