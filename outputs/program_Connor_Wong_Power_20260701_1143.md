## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor Wong's primary deficits revolve around poor trunk lateral movement and pelvic obliquity at release, coupled with lower humerus angular velocity and lower-half power output (PPU vel at PP, PPU time to RPD max s). The three-day plyo cycle is designed to progressively address these issues by emphasizing hip drive, trunk rotation, and overall intent. The Velocity Phase allows for high-intensity plyos up to P3, but given the in-season Saturday game schedule, the weekly layout prioritizes recovery around the game, using P0 for recovery, P1 for hybrid days, and no P2 plyo block to avoid over-stressing an active in-season pitcher.
