## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Ethan's primary deficits are focused on lead leg force production (vertical and resultant peak forces) and horizontal abduction mobility, which impact his ability to drive off the mound and efficiently rotate. This three-day plyo cycle, designed for his 'Velocity Phase,' aims to progressively address these issues. P0 focuses on recovery and fundamental connection, minimizing stress. P1 gradually builds intent, introducing drills that emphasize hip drive and initial lead leg loading. P2 is a high-intensity day, aggressively targeting lead leg block and maximizing rotational velocity and arm speed to close the identified gaps, leveraging the 3 oz ball for intent education. The weekly layout adheres strictly to the 'Saturday game day' cadence, ensuring sufficient recovery while providing the necessary stimuli for velocity development.
