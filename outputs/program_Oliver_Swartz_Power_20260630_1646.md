## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Oliver Swartz's plyo cycle is designed to primarily address his significant deficits in kinematic sequence timing (pitch_force_peak_v_to_b_lag_ms) and hip drive (pitch_max_pelvis_linear_vel_mph). The sessions progressively increase in intensity, allowing for recovery and feel on P0, balanced application on P1, and maximum intent and targeted correction on P2.
