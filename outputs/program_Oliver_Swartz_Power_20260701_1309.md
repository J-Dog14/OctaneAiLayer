## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Oliver Swartz focuses on improving his significant deficit in pelvis-to-trunk velocity lag and enhancing hip drive, both critical for his 'Power' focus in the Velocity Phase. The P0 and P1 sessions prioritize feel, connection, and gradual re-patterning, with drills like Throw to Reverse Throw and OG Pivots building foundational movement. The P2 session aggressively targets these inefficiencies with lighter balls and higher reps in drills like Janitors and Drop Steps, aiming for maximum intent expression and powerful kinematic sequencing, while respecting the in-season structure with P1 on game day and P0 on structural rest days.
