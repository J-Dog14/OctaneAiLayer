## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Brett Jordan's plyo cycle focuses on addressing his Proteus D2 extension velocity and acceleration deficits, which point to a need for improved hip drive and rotational power. The feel goal is to enhance connection through the kinetic chain and maximize intent. As he is in the 'Velocity Phase', the off-season cadence is applied, allowing for a P2 work day mid-week to push intent and specific inefficiency correction.
