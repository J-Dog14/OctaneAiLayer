## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Ethan Dumar's primary deficit is identified as lead leg force production and stiffness, indicated by low Z-scores in `pitch_force_lead_peak_vertical` and `screen_dj_rsi`. A secondary deficit is horizontal abduction mobility. This three-day cycle prioritizes drills that enhance lead leg drive, ground force application, and hip-trunk rotation. As Ethan is a Starter in the Velocity Phase, the program balances recovery (P0) with targeted work (P1, P2) to express intent and correct inefficiencies, without any phase-based downgrades. Each session incorporates a priming reverse throw, builds intensity through rotational and lead leg specific drills, and scales volume and intensity appropriate for the plyo level.
