## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Luke Bryant, a starter in the Velocity Phase, prioritizes correcting his dominant deficits: low hand angular velocity, insufficient lead leg ground force, and weak rotational acceleration. The Velocity Phase allows for higher intensity, enabling aggressive targeting of these issues, particularly at the P2 level. The P0 and P1 sessions progressively build foundational movements and feel, while P2 maximizes intent and power transfer through specific drills. Each session balances intent with recovery needs, ensuring Luke can push his velocity ceiling while maintaining arm health and efficient movement patterns throughout his training week.
