## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Bryson Pope is designed to address his primary deficits in total trunk flexion, peak abduction, and maximum horizontal abduction, with a focus on improving hip drive, lead leg mechanics, and a more efficient arm path. As he is in the Velocity Phase, all plyo levels (P0, P1, P2) are permitted without phase-based downgrades. The P0 session prioritizes recovery and gentle pattern reinforcement. P1 introduces balanced feel with moderate intent, while P2 is geared towards maximal intent expression and aggressive correction of his identified biomechanical gaps to translate to higher velocity on the mound.
