## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Isaiah Pinero's primary deficit is a significant lack of lead leg force production, specifically vertical force, as indicated by his -2.93 Z-score in `pitch_force_lead_peak_vertical_pct_fc_br` and related metrics. This is further supported by general Proteus power/velocity deficits. The plyo cycle prioritizes drills that enhance lead leg blocking, hip drive, and kinetic chain connection. As Isaiah is in the 'Velocity Phase' and has a Saturday game day, the weekly layout adheres to the in-season cadence where the game itself serves as the high-intent (P2) stimulus, and the plyo block on game day is P1. The P0 and P1 sessions progressively rebuild intent and address the lead leg and hip deficits with increasing intensity.
