## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Max Boyatt, a college starter in the Velocity Phase, is designed to address his primary pitching mechanics deficits: insufficient elbow flexion at max external rotation, delayed pelvis rotation stop, and low lead leg impulse. The Velocity Phase allows for high-intent work, so no downgrades from the recommended plyo levels (P0, P1, P2) are necessary. The cycle strategically integrates drills across intensities, with P0 prioritizing tissue recovery and feel, P1 balancing feel with light intent to build patterns, and P2 aggressively targeting his inefficiencies with maximal intent and movement correction. The weekly layout balances work and recovery, crucial for a starter maintaining high velocity in this phase.
