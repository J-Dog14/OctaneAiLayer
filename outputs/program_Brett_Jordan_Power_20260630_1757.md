## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Brett Jordan targets his Proteus D2 extension deficits, which indicate a need for improved pelvis-trunk amplification and kinematic sequence timing. The cycle progresses from P0 recovery, focusing on tissue restoration and basic feel, to P1 for balanced intent and pattern reinforcement, culminating in P2 for maximal intent expression and direct correction of rotational power and lead leg mechanics. Being in the Velocity Phase, the program leverages higher-intensity drills to build arm speed while maintaining a structured recovery to prevent overload, aligning with his starter role and Saturday game schedule.
