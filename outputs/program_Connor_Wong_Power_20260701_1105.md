## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor Wong presents with significant deficits in `pitch_trunk_lateral_at_release` and `pitch_pelvis_obliquity_at_release`, indicating inefficient rotational mechanics, alongside lower `pitch_humerus_ang_vel_max`. The cycle is designed to progressively address these by emphasizing hip-shoulder separation, controlled pelvic rotation, and building arm speed through increasing intent.
