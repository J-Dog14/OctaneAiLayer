## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Clay Haigh, a college starter in the Velocity Phase, presents with a notable deficit in reactive strength (DJ RSI Z=-0.98). This plyo cycle is designed to address this by progressively introducing more elastic and high-intent drills through the week, focusing on lead leg drive and hip-shoulder separation for improved kinetic chain efficiency. As he is in the Velocity Phase, high-intent P2 days are fully supported, but P0 and P1 days ensure adequate recovery and feel development, laying a durable foundation for velocity gains. The cycle balances recovery with targeted work to build an explosive delivery while mitigating fatigue.
