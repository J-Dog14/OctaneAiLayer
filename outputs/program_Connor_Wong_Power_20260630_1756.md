## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor Wong, a starter in the Velocity Phase, presents significant deficits in trunk lateral flexion and pelvis obliquity at release, coupled with lower shotput acceleration on Proteus, indicating a need for improved rotational power and hip-shoulder separation. This three-day cycle prioritizes drills that enhance kinematic sequencing, trunk rotation, and overall intent. The Velocity Phase allows for aggressive P2 work, which we leverage to push intent and directly target these biomechanical gaps, while P0 and P1 days focus on progressive feel and moderate intensity to ensure recovery and build capacity.
