## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor Wong's plyo cycle prioritizes addressing his significant deficits in trunk lateral at release, pelvis obliquity, and humerus angular velocity, which indicate a need for improved rotational power, hip drive, and arm speed. The Velocity Phase allows for higher intensity, so P2 days will push intent and specific corrective movements. P0 and P1 days will focus on recovery and gradual build-up of feel and light intent, respectively, before hitting the max-intent work. The cycle aims to enhance his kinematic sequence and force transfer from the lower half through the trunk to the arm.
