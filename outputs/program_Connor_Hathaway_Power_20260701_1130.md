## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Connor Hathaway's primary deficits revolve around lower-body power generation and velocity expression, specifically in d2 extension (hip drive) and jump metrics (overall power output). The feel goal for this cycle is to enhance hip drive and overall intent. The P0 session focuses on feel and tissue restoration, introducing light hip rotation and arm path work. P1 gradually ramps up, incorporating more targeted hip rotation and lead leg drills to begin addressing the power deficit. P2 aggressively targets hip drive, kinematic sequence timing, and max intent, leveraging lighter balls to encourage higher velocity expression. This cycle, given Connor is in the Velocity Phase, is designed to progressively build toward expressing maximum intent and transferring these power gains to his throwing mechanics.
