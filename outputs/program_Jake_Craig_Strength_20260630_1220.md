## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Rebuild Capacity

> Jake Craig, a starter in the Rebuild Capacity phase, presents significant deficits in pelvis angular velocity, hip-shoulder separation, and rotational power (shotput metrics). The plyo cycle prioritizes drills that enhance rotational explosiveness and kinetic sequencing, aiming for a 'hip drive' and 'intent' feel. Due to the 'Rebuild Capacity' annual phase, the P2 session's volume and intensity are intentionally moderated to function as a high-end P1, respecting the phase's guardrails for arm health and durability while still introducing movements targeted at inefficiency correction. The weekly layout reflects this phase constraint, limiting true 'work' days to the highest allowed intensity (P1 in this phase).
