## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Oliver Swartz's plyo cycle prioritizes addressing his significant deficits in kinematic sequence timing (pelvis-trunk lag), low pelvis linear velocity (hip drive), and limited external rotation, alongside lead leg acceleration.
