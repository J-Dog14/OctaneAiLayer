## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Isaiah Pinero's primary deficits are centered around lead leg block and force generation (low vertical/resultant force at lead leg peak) coupled with Proteus data suggesting a need for improved rotational power and velocity. The three-day cycle is designed to progressively address these. P0 focuses on recovery and foundational movement patterns. P1 introduces light intent and targeted hip/lead leg engagement. P2 is a full work day, maximizing intent expression to drive velocity and aggressively correct lead leg force generation and rotational power deficits, leveraging the 'Velocity Phase' annual context to push intent.
