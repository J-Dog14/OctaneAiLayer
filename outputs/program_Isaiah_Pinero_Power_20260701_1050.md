## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Isaiah Pinero's primary deficit is a lack of lead leg vertical force production, complemented by lower Proteus extension velocity and shotput power. The P0 session focuses on fundamental connection and tissue restoration, lightly touching on hip and arm path. The P1 session gradually introduces more dynamic lead leg engagement and rotational power. The P2 session aggressively targets lead leg blocking mechanics and intent, leveraging lighter balls and drills that maximize extension velocity to address both his 3D mechanics and Proteus deficits. Given he is in the Velocity Phase, high-intent work is appropriate, and all selected plyo levels (P0, P1, P2) are permitted.
