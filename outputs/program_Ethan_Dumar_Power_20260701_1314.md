## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Ethan Dumar primarily targets his significant deficit in lead leg vertical force production and also addresses limited hip horizontal abduction. The P0 and P1 sessions focus on re-establishing kinetic chain connection and hip drive through feel-oriented, heavier ball drills. The P2 session aggressively pushes lead leg block and hip rotation with higher-intent, lighter ball drills to maximize power transfer, leveraging the Velocity Phase's allowance for high-intent work. The weekly cadence reflects an in-season starter with Saturday game days, prioritizing recovery after the game and moderate intensity mid-week, with the game itself serving as the primary high-intensity event.
