> This three-day plyo cycle for Connor Chicoli, a college starter in the Velocity Phase, primarily targets his deficits in trunk flexion and lead leg force production, as well as improving D2 extension velocity identified in Proteus data. The cycle aims to enhance hip drive, lead leg blocking, and overall intent, progressively building intensity from P0 recovery to P2 high-intent work.
