## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Max Boyatt, a starter in the Velocity Phase with Saturday games, is designed to address his primary deficits in elbow flexion at max ER, pelvis rotation stop time, and lead leg impulse. The Velocity Phase allows for higher intent, including P3, but P3 is reserved only for explicit intent education and not assigned by schedule. Therefore, the cycle incorporates increasing intensity from P0 to P2, with P2 focusing on max intent expression and direct deficit correction. Each session includes drills that prime connection, enhance hip-shoulder separation, improve lead leg force transfer, and refine arm path, aligning with his biomechanical gaps and the phase's velocity development goal. The weekly layout adheres strictly to the single-P2-per-week rule (on game day) and mandatory post-game P0 days, ensuring adequate recovery and progressive intent building.
