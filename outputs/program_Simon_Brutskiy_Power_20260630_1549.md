## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Simon Brutskiy, a starter in the Velocity Phase, prioritizes correcting his pitching mechanics deficits in pelvis obliquity, horizontal abduction, and pelvic angular velocity, while also enhancing intent. Given his role as a starter, the aim is to maximize volume within the provided drill constraints. The limited drill pools for P1 and P2, however, result in lower exercise counts than typically prescribed for a starter, a point that should be noted for future drill pool expansion. The cycle progresses from tissue restoration (P0) to moderate intent (P1) and finally high-intent work (P2), aligning with the Velocity Phase's goal of developing arm speed and intent.
