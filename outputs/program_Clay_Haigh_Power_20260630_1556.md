## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Clay Haigh, a college starter in the Velocity Phase, presents deficits in drop jump reactive strength index (DJ RSI) and single-leg vertical jump height, indicating a need for improved elastic energy utilization and lead leg drive. This three-day plyo cycle is designed to address these deficits with increasing intensity. P0 focuses on recovery and foundational movement, P1 introduces more specific reactive strength and lead leg drills, and P2 culminates in high-intent work that pushes hip drive, lead leg blocking, and overall power transfer, aligning with the Velocity Phase's goal of developing arm speed and velocity ceiling.
