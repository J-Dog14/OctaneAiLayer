## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Brett Jordan's plyo cycle focuses on improving lower body power (CMJ deficits) and efficient energy transfer (D2 extension deficits) by emphasizing hip drive and lead leg stability. As a high school starter in the Velocity Phase, the P0 and P1 sessions prioritize feel, connection, and gradual intent, while the P2 session pushes maximal intent for velocity development and targeted inefficiency correction. Although the Velocity Phase allows P2, the in-season weekly layout still follows the 'game-as-P2-event' rule, so the P2 plyo session serves as a template for off-season work or dedicated mid-week pen days if the athlete were a reliever.
