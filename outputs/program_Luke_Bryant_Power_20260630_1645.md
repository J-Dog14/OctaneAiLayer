## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Luke Bryant, a starter in the Velocity Phase, focuses primarily on addressing his lead leg ground reaction force (GRF) and Proteus shotput acceleration deficits. The Velocity Phase permits high-intensity work, so P2 days push intent and correction, while P0 and P1 days provide necessary recovery and progressive re-introduction of movement patterns. The cycle aims to improve his lower half stability, lead leg block, and rotational power, which should contribute to better force transfer and overall pitching velocity, alongside refining arm path and kinematic sequencing.
