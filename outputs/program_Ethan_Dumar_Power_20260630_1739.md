## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Ethan Dumar's plyo cycle is designed to primarily address his significant lead leg force production deficits (vertical and resultant peak forces) and secondary mobility issues in horizontal abduction, all within the context of his 'Velocity Phase' annual training block. The P0 and P1 sessions focus on re-patterning and building foundational strength for his lead leg and arm path, ensuring proper connection and feel. The P2 session aggressively targets lead leg force and intent through dynamic drills, while also integrating high-velocity arm path work to optimize his kinematic sequence for increased velocity, respecting the phase's intent-driven goals.
