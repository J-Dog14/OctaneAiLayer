## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Simon Brutskiy, a starter in the Velocity Phase, presents with key deficits in pelvis rotation (obliquity and angular velocity) and maximum horizontal abduction, alongside a need for greater shotput acceleration. This three-day plyo cycle directly targets these areas, emphasizing hip drive, core-pelvis connection, and explosive intent. Due to limitations in the provided candidate drill pools, the P1 and P2 sessions contain fewer unique drill types than typical guidelines, a constraint that coaches should be aware of. The overall aim is to leverage the Velocity Phase's allowance for high-intensity work to close movement gaps and enhance power, while still integrating critical recovery and hybrid days.
