## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> This three-day plyo cycle for Ryan Chasse, a Starter in the Velocity Phase, is designed to aggressively target his dominant biomechanical deficits in hip-shoulder separation and trunk rotation while enhancing his Proteus power/velocity output. The Velocity Phase allows for maximal intent, so the P2 day is heavily weighted toward high-intensity drills to address these gaps directly. P0 and P1 days provide crucial recovery and progressive build-up, ensuring continuity in feel and movement quality throughout the week, with a focus on rotational patterns and arm timing.
