## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Barron Mcconnell's plyo cycle targets his primary deficits in pelvis angular velocity and elbow flexion at maximum external rotation, which point to issues with hip drive and arm path/connection. As a starter in the Velocity Phase, the plan prioritizes high-intent expression and specific movement correction without stacking P2 work on game day. The cycle progresses from P0 recovery with tissue restoration, through P1 for balanced feel and light intent, to P2 for maximum intent and focused inefficiency correction, allowing for skill development while respecting the in-season weekly demands.
