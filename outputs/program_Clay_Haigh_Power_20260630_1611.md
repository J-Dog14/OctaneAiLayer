## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Clay Haigh's deficit profile highlights lower body reactive strength and single-leg power (DJ RSI, SLV, PPU). The programming throughout this cycle prioritizes lead leg mechanics, hip drive, and overall intent, with increased emphasis on these areas at higher plyo levels. As a starter in the Velocity Phase, all P0-P2 levels are permitted, allowing for direct correction of deficits during work days while ensuring adequate recovery and feel-based training on off-days. This structure aims to develop explosive power and efficient energy transfer to support velocity gains.
