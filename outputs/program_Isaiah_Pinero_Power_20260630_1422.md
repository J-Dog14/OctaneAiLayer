## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Isaiah Pinero's primary biomechanical deficits are related to lead leg vertical force production at foot contact and overall lead leg resultant force. Given Isaiah is a starter in the Velocity Phase, the cycle prioritizes maximizing intent and addressing these lead leg and rotational power inefficiencies, while ensuring adequate recovery through varied plyo intensities.
