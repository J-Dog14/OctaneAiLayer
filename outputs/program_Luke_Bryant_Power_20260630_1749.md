## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Luke Bryant's plyo cycle prioritizes addressing his deficits in hand angular velocity and lead leg GRF, along with improving shotput acceleration (rotational power). The Velocity Phase allows for full expression up to P2, which means we can aggressively target these inefficiencies. P0 days focus on recovery and foundational movement patterns, P1 days introduce light intent while still emphasizing feel and pattern restoration, and P2 days maximize intent and specific inefficiency correction, particularly for lead leg block and rotational whip. The cycle ensures a progressive ramp-up and crucial recovery periods around his Saturday game schedule, adhering to phase-based guardrails.
