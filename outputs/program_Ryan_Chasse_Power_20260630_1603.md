## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Ryan Chasse's primary mechanical deficits center around hip-shoulder separation and trunk rotation relative to the pelvis. The plyo cycle aims to specifically address these gaps by prioritizing drills that foster better rotational sequencing, hip-trunk dissociation, and arm external rotation timing. As Ryan is in the Velocity Phase, the program pushes for higher intent and volume on P2 days, while P0 and P1 days provide necessary recovery and feel-based reinforcement, ensuring long-term durability while chasing velocity gains. The structure builds progressively, with P0 focused on restoration, P1 on balanced intent, and P2 on maximum expression of rotational power.
