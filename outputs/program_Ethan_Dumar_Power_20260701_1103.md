## Plyo / Throwing — 3-day cycle · Starter · phase: Velocity Phase

> Ethan Dumar's primary deficit lies in lead leg force production, specifically in vertical and resultant force at front foot contact to ball release. This indicates a need to improve lead leg block, hip drive, and overall lower body connection to transfer energy efficiently. A secondary deficit in horizontal abduction mobility also suggests opportunities to improve arm path and hip rotation. This three-day cycle prioritizes building lead leg awareness and power through progressively intense drills, while also incorporating drills that promote hip drive and improve horizontal abduction. The Velocity Phase allows for full intensity up to P2, which is leveraged on game day, with structured recovery and progressive intent building on other days.
